## Vesting & Lockups (High Level)

**Investors (10%)**

- Short vesting depending on tier (example structures):  
  - 50% at TGE + 50% after 1 month  
  - 40% at TGE + 60% over 2 months  
  - 35% at TGE + 65% over 3 months  

**Team & Strategic (2% total)**

- Vested linearly over ~12 months.
- No TGE unlock.

**Promo (5%)**

- 3% vested over 12 months.  
- 2% used more flexibly around launch and early growth.

**Community, Ecosystem, Treasury**

- Distributed gradually over time through campaigns, app rewards, and long-term initiatives.

---

## Design Goals

- **Fast but structured:** Short investor vesting suitable for meme coin culture, but long-term alignment for team and promo.
- **Community-first:** Significant allocations for rewards, quests, and in-app incentives.
- **Transparent:** Token allocations and vesting logic are openly documented and updated here as they finalize.
