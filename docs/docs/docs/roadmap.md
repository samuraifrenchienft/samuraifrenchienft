# Roadmap – SamuraiFrenchieNFT (High Level)

This roadmap focuses on major phases. Timelines may change with market conditions and community feedback.

---

## Phase 1 – Foundations

- Establish brand and lore (Samurai Frenchie, Shadow Litter, frog ninjas).
- Create base visual identity (logo, banners, PFP style).
- Launch and brand:
  - Website
  - Twitter/X
  - Discord
  - Telegram
- Start early community building and content.

---

## Phase 2 – NFT Collection Launch

- Finalize NFT art, traits, and metadata.
- Define supply, mint price, and mint structure (WL/public).
- Secure listings on NFT calendars and drop platforms.
- Launch the SamuraiFrenchieNFT collection.
- Integrate NFTs into:
  - Community roles
  - Lore content
  - Early access/benefits

---

## Phase 3 – Meme Coin Launch (Base)

- Finalize tokenomics and vesting schedules.
- Integrate with a launchpad for SAFT, deployment, and claim UI.
- Execute TGE on Base.
- Seed initial DEX liquidity.
- Clearly communicate lockups and vesting to the community.

---

## Phase 4 – App MVP (Duel/Game Concept)

- Design and build a simple duel/battle MVP:
  - Wallet connect
  - Core duel loop
  - Basic NFT + token integration
- Run a closed alpha with the community.
- Expand into a small open beta and iterate.

---

## Phase 5 – Growth & Expansion

- Scale marketing via KOLs, YouTubers, and community collaborations.
- Expand quests and on-chain missions via Zealy / similar tools.
- Add new modes, content, and features to the app.
- Explore additional drops and ecosystem partnerships.

Roadmap will be updated here as milestones are completed.
